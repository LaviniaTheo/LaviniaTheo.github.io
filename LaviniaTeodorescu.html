ABOUT ME

Student at ICHB | President of Interact Bucharest

Raising senior at ICHB, Romania, with passion for people and entrepreneurship. Throughout my 17 years journey I tried to expand my horizonts and make the most out of everything that I was offered.

I began dancing at 4 years old, and now, 13 years later, I am practicing folk and contemporary dance.

My main interest is organic chemistry, the science behind every living organism. I am currently a national Olympic, and I hope to continue studying chemistry in the future years by combining it with dermatology.

In the past 4 years I had the pleasure to be part of the amazing team that is Interact Bucharest. This month I've just begun my presidency, and I am currently trying to maximize our projects' impact and value.
